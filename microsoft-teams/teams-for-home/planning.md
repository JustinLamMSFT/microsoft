# Connect with anyone, anytime

Microsoft Teams is free for personal use—no subscription needed. Sign in to schedule and join video calls for up to 60 minutes, chat, share files and photos, and collaborate with your community.

[Sign in](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?linkid=2269735&clcid=0x409&culture=en-us&country=us) [Download the Teams app](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-teams/download-app)
