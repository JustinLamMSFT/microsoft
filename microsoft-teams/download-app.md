- 01/
    
    ### What is happening to the classic Teams app?
    
    The classic Teams app is being replaced by the new Teams app, which is a faster, more reliable, and more secure version of the app.
    
- 02/
    
    ### How can I switch to the new Teams app?
    
    To switch to the new Teams app, use the in-app toggle that will appear on the top of your screen, or download and install the app.
    
- 03/
    
    ### What are the benefits of the new Teams app?
    
    The new Teams app offers many benefits. You can have all your Teams accounts in a single app—whether they’re for work, school, or personal use—and easily switch between them without signing out and signing in again. Additionally, you can access new features, such as enhanced chat, calling, and meeting experiences.
    
- 04/
    
    ### Do I need to install the Teams app to be able to use Teams? 
    
    No. You can access Teams using your web browser. Find Teams on the web at https://teams.microsoft.com.
