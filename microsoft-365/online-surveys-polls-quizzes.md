## Easily create custom surveys, polls, and quizzes

- ### 
    
    Let built-in intelligence work for you
    
    Collect the right feedback for your goals, no matter your skill level. Built-in AI and smart recommendations do the heavy lifting for you.
    
    ![A computer screen showing a feedback form titled "Studio 8 all hands meeting feedback"](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/accordion-4-374629?resMode=sharp2&op_usm=1.5,0.65,15,0&wid=1664&hei=1062&qlt=100&fmt=png-alpha&fit=constrain)
    
- ### 
    
    Increase your reach
    
    Seamlessly use Forms with your favorite apps to connect with audiences where they are. Easily share surveys to collaborate across groups. Ensure everyone’s voice is heard with multilingual support.
    
    ![Three people sit at a conference table, engaged in a meeting. One person writes in a notebook.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/3jafacdz-2-accordion-2-800x513?resMode=sharp2&op_usm=1.5,0.65,15,0&wid=1664&hei=1062&qlt=100&fmt=png-alpha&fit=constrain)
    
- ### 
    
    Quickly turn insights into actions
    
    Visualize data in seconds with powerful, real-time charts and automatically generated reports. Easily export to Excel for customized, in-depth analysis.
    
    ![Child in a floral shirt using a tablet while lying on a bed in a bright room.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/accordion-3-374629?resMode=sharp2&op_usm=1.5,0.65,15,0&wid=1664&hei=1062&qlt=100&fmt=png-alpha&fit=constrain)
    
- ### 
    
    Stay engaged on the go
    
    Remain connected no matter where you are—Forms works from any web browser, on any device.
