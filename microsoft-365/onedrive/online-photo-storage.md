## Keep your favorite memories nearby

- ### 
    
    Save space on your phone
    
    Enjoy enough space to store all the memorable moments from birthdays, vacations, fun nights out, anniversaries, graduations, and weddings, freeing up space on your phone.

A bowl of oranges and a potted plant are on the table.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/qsoux796-2-accordion-3-800x513?resMode=sharp2&op_usm=1.5,0.65,15,0&wid=1664&hei=1062&qlt=100&fmt=png-alpha&fit=constrain)
