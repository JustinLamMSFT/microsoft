Overview

## Store, back up, and secure all your files

- ### 
    
    Effortless backup
    
    Seamlessly back up your files to the cloud. Set up PC folder backup, and OneDrive will automatically back up and sync all the files in your Desktop, Documents, and Pictures folders.
    
    [Learn more](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkID=2120912&clcid=0x409&culture=en-us&country=us)
    
    ![A laptop displaying a screen with options to back up folders.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/accordion-1-375379?resMode=sharp2&op_usm=1.5,0.65,15,0&wid=1664&hei=1062&qlt=100&fmt=png-alpha&fit=constrain)
    
- ### 
    
    ### Access your PC files without your PC
    
    Your backed-up PC folders are available online and in the OneDrive mobile app, so you can view or edit files on the go.
    
    [Get the mobile app](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/onedrive/mobile)
    
    ![A woman stands in a kitchen, holding a smartphone and looking at the screen. A clock and abstract art hang on the wall.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/8z281tcw-2-accordion-2-800x513?resMode=sharp2&op_usm=1.5,0.65,15,0&wid=1664&hei=1062&qlt=100&fmt=png-alpha&fit=constrain)
    
- ### 
    
    ### Undo changes with version history
    
    Easily view and restore previous versions of your OneDrive files from the last 30 days.
    
    [Learn more](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkID=2121201&clcid=0x409&culture=en-us&country=us)
    
    ![A person interacts with version history on a laptop screen, navigating through listed dates and actions.](https://cdn-dynmedia-1.microsoft.com/is/image/microsoftcorp/cbgdwk1d-2-accordion-3-800x513?resMode=sharp2&op_usm=1.5,0.65,15,0&wid=1664&hei=1062&qlt=100&fmt=png-alpha&fit=constrain)
    
- ### 
    
    ### Keep your most important documents secured
    
    Help protect digital copies of important documents and sensitive files in the Personal Vault. Secured by identity verification, the OneDrive mobile app can scan documents directly into your vault.
    
    [Learn more](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?LinkID=2120912&clcid=0x409&culture=en-us&country=us)
